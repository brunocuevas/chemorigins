Modules

What are modules?

Modules are groups of chemical reactions. They enable building prebiotic chemical pathways, reporting the chemical hypothesis behind a specific experiment, or simply enumerating reactions connected by any criteria.

How are they built?

A module can be defined only as a list of chemical reaction identifiers. Check the documentation to find information about how to define your own modules.
